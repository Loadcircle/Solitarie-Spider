Privacy Policy

Spider Solitaire (provisional name) — Last updated: Feb. 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Spider Solitaire Team (“we”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use Spider Solitaire (the “App”).

Quick summary: The App is offline and does not require an account. However, the App displays ads and offers an in-app purchase to remove them. As a result, some third-party providers (e.g. ad networks and app stores) may process technical data required to operate those services.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the App, we may collect or process:

  • Game data (local): progress, statistics, settings, streaks, or similar data stored on your device.
  • Technical data: device and app information (device model, OS version, language), basic logs, and crash/error events.
  • Advertising identifiers: advertising or installation identifiers (depending on your device, region, and settings) used to show and measure ads, prevent fraud, and limit ad frequency.
  • Purchase information: purchase status/confirmation for the “remove ads” feature (transactions are handled by the app store).

Note: We do not require you to create an account and do not ask for personal details such as your name or email to use the App (unless you voluntarily contact us for support).

2. How We Use the Information

  • To operate the App (save local progress, settings, and core gameplay).
  • To display ads and measure their performance (impressions, clicks, fraud prevention).
  • To enable and validate the in-app purchase to remove ads (purchase state/restoration).
  • To improve stability and performance (crash and error diagnostics).
  • For security and service integrity (abuse or anomalous activity detection).

3. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services for advertising, purchases, and stability, such as:

  • Advertising: Google AdMob (or another ad network) to display ads.
  • Purchases: Google Play / Apple App Store to process in-app purchases.
  • Crash reporting (optional): tools such as Firebase Crashlytics to detect and fix crashes.
  • Analytics (optional): analytics tools such as Firebase Analytics, if enabled.

These providers may collect technical data required to provide their services (e.g. device identifiers, installation identifiers, crash events, or ad measurement data), in accordance with their own privacy policies.

4. Advertising

The App displays advertisements. Depending on your country, device settings, and available permissions, ads may be non-personalized or personalized. You can manage ad-related preferences through your device’s system settings.

5. In-App Purchases (Remove Ads)

The App offers a one-time purchase to remove ads. Purchases are processed through Google Play or the Apple App Store. We do not store full payment details; these are handled by the respective platform. We may store or verify the purchase status to unlock the feature and allow restorations when applicable.

6. Data Retention

Game progress and settings are generally stored locally on your device. Technical data collected by third-party services is retained according to those providers’ retention policies.

7. Data Deletion

Since the App is offline, you can delete game data by clearing the App’s storage from your device settings or by uninstalling the App (which typically removes local data).
